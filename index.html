I am a finance and management professional with deep roots in fintech operations. Over the years, I have built a strong understanding of financial systems, reconciliation workflows, and the operational backbone that keeps modern financial platforms running.

My work in fintech sparked a genuine fascination with the technology that powers it — which led me to embark on a structured journey into software engineering. I am currently studying Python, web development, and full-stack engineering through FreeCodeCamp's curriculum.

My ambition is to grow into an engineer capable of building platforms that help companies and community groups scale their operations through thoughtful, accessible technology. I believe the best digital tools are built by people who understand both the human problem and the technical solution.

This site is both my professional portfolio and an open journal of that learning journey — written in public, updated with every milestone.
